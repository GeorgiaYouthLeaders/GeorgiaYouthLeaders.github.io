Our Mission

We strive to bring change-makers together and empower teens to change their community and the world. Through mentorships, networking, and youth development we are helping take teen projects to the next level. We strive to help teens identify injustices in their community, fiscally sponsor service projects and create an association of changemakers! We also take in students looking for ways to get involved in helping the world.
