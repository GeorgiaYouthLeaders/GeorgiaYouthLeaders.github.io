About Us

Our Mission

We strive to bring change-makers together and empower teens to change their community and the world. Through mentorships, networking, and youth development we are helping take teen projects to the next level. We strive to help teens identify injustices in their community, fiscally sponsor service projects and create an association of changemakers! We also take in students looking for ways to get involved in helping the world.

What We Do

Becoming a 501(c)(3) can be a very complicated and time consuming process, not to mention the amount of money it takes to become one. Georgia Youth Leaders works lift that burden from students who want to create change. We are a 501(c)(3) that will fiscally sponsor projects! Our goal is to develop the next generation of socially conscious leaders, create servant leaders and help young people change the world!
